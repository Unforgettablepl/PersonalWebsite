## ZIO 2023

## Qualifying cutoff

* Std 12: 40
* Std 11: 35
* Std 10: 30
* Std 9: 25
* Std 8 and below: 20

The cutoff score is relaxed by 5 marks for female students in each category.

## ZIO 2025


## Qualifying cutoff

### Male students
- Std 12: 40
- Std 11: 40
- Std 10: 35
- Std 9: 30
- Std 8: 25
- Std 7 and below: 20

### Female students
- Std 12: 25
- Std 11: 25
- Std 10: 25
- Std 9: 20
- Std 8: 20
- Std 7 and below: 20
